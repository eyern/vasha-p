Talk to Us

Do I need prior experience?

No. Tandem flights are instructional and fully managed by your certified instructor.

What should I wear?

Wear closed-toe shoes (sneakers or hiking boots). Dress according to the weather, but bring a light windbreaker or jacket, as it can be cooler in the air.

How safe is paragliding?

Paragliding is considered extremely safe. Equipment includes multiple secure harness points, and our instructors are licensed professionals with years of experience
